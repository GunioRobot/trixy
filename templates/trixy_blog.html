    The Living Is Easy

    This page about the Brain has several levels of explanation. As in, you can choose to read the novice, intermediate, or expert version.

    Of course, this feature is as old as the educational video game industry.

    But what if, like any other microformat, difficulty levels were to be standardized, such as in my new favorite software of the moment: Ontological MediaWiki. Too bad I can’t find any semantic markup for describing levels of discourse.

    Something like this would be tres cool:


    
 <living difficulty="easy"> 
     Summertime 

</living>


    I wonder what would happen if this additional format were to be widely introduced to ontologies…
