About Plopquiz

Each Plopquiz contains self-generating, self-updating quiz items. Smart quizzes for smart people.

These quizzes provide a simple way to assess and share accurate proficiency data about the emerging knowledge domains that haven't had a chance to complete (or start!) the process of being institutionalized.


How It Works

To generate the Co-Founder Quiz, we provided a list of keywords specifying the proficiencies we seek in a hire, such as "Django", "Statistical Analysis", "Carbon" and "Sustainability".

Because these proficiencies haven't been standardized, Plopquiz graphs these proficiencies, and crafts quiz items using material already published on the web.
