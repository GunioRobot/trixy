Are you interested in working for a well funded (top tier Silicon Valley VC) Company, with real revenues, working on cutting edge products that can be used every day by millions of people? VirtuOz is a leading provider of Virtual Agent solutions. A virtual agent (VA) is software built to chat automatically with Internet users. Based on syntactic and semantic analysis, it extracts the meaning of users’ input, compares it to a knowledge base, and provides an appropriate response. Our clients (eBay, PayPal, American Express, etc.) use their virtual agents for customer support, cross selling, advertising, and more. Our Company is currently based in Redwood City, California as well as Paris, France, and we are opening an office in downtown NYC. We are currently looking for high energy individuals join our Operations department. VirtuOz is a cutting-edge start-up offering great clients and projects with rapid deployment. You won’t be bored! Senior Project Manager Responsibilities The Project Manager will report to the local office manager and will work closely with the Global Operations Department. He/she will be responsible for: - Acting as the principal VirtuOz contact on key VA projects - Analyzing client needs and expectations. - Developing project goals and a project plan to achieve them - Ensuring that the various phases of VA development run smoothly, including VA user interface development, content integration, metrics, quality control, etc. - Organizing VA training and improvements. - Providing analysis of VA activity. - Directing a content management team - Achieving a high level of customer satisfaction Required Skills - University graduate with minimum 5 years work experience in project management. - Outstanding written and verbal communication skills. - Willing to travel (Europe and the US) for meetings with clients. - Ability to engage in technical discussions with clients and internal team about project requirements and challenges and to drive resolution - Proficiency in MS Office applications and ability to quickly learn in-house software. Preferred Skills - Project management experience in a software or web application environment - Experience deploying or integrating with CRM applications - 8 years project management - French or Spanish (notions) Content Specialist/Manager Responsibilities The Content Manager will report to the Project Manager. He/she will be responsible for: - Creating and updating the VA knowledge base. - Acting as a principal contact for client content. - Creating personalized metrics in the VA statistical interface. - Organizing VA training and improvements. - Providing quality control and content management after project launch. Required Skills - University graduate with 0-3 years work experience - Outstanding written and verbal communication skills. - Proven ability to learn quickly. - Self starter with proven organizational and problem-solving skills and attention to detail. - Technical ability: comfortable working with multiple desktop applications and able to quickly learn in-house software. - Comfortable working in a fast-paced environment. Preferred Skills - Computer minded - Previous customer support experience - Previous experience in content management - French or Spanish (notions: fluency is not a must) Still interested? Great! Here is something else: if you are selected, you will come to Paris this summer (housing+perks paid) for training before moving back to California in September!
